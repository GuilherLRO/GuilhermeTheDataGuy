# Product Hierarchy Documentation

**Scope:** Apparel, footwear, fashion accessories, bags, and jewelry.

---

## Accessories

### Ties, Tie Clips & Pocket Squares

**Definition:** Formal and semi-formal menswear accessories worn around the neck or used to complement dress shirts, including decorative pocket accessories.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Neckwear:** necktie, silk tie, bow tie, formal tie, dress tie
- **Western Style:** bolo tie, western tie, leather bolo, silver bolo
- **Complementary Items:** pocket square, tie clip, tie bar
- **Patterns/Materials:** solid, striped, paisley, silk, satin, woven

**Key Characteristics:** Worn as part of formal or business attire; made from silk, satin, or leather; often sold in coordinated sets with pocket squares or cufflinks.

---

### Umbrellas

**Definition:** Personal rain and sun protection accessories that are portable and carried by hand.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Standard:** compact umbrella, folding umbrella, travel umbrella, automatic umbrella
- **Sport/Oversized:** golf umbrella, vented canopy umbrella, dual-canopy umbrella
- **Features:** windproof, waterproof, auto-open, UV protection, telescoping

**Key Characteristics:** Portable, handheld weather protection; includes compact travel sizes and larger golf umbrellas designed for personal use.

---

### Hats

**Definition:** Headwear for fashion, sun protection, warmth, sports activities, or work.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Casual:** baseball cap, trucker hat, snapback, dad hat, mesh cap
- **Cold Weather:** beanie, watch cap, knit cap, fleece hat, cuffed beanie, pom-pom beanie
- **Western:** cowboy hat, straw hat, felt hat, bangora, rancher hat
- **Sports/Protective:** football helmet, batting helmet, ski helmet

**Key Characteristics:** Head coverings for various purposes including casual wear, cold weather protection, western lifestyle, and sports safety.

---

### Hair Accessories

**Definition:** Items designed for styling, securing, or decorating human hair.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Fasteners:** hair clip, barrette, hair claw, alligator clip, bobby pin
- **Elastics:** hair tie, scrunchie, ponytail holder, elastic hair band
- **Decorative:** hair bow, ribbon bow, embellished clip, concho clip
- **Bands:** headband, head tie, head wrap

**Key Characteristics:** Accessories worn in or on human hair for styling, securing, or decoration; ranges from functional elastics to decorative clips and bows.

---

### Belts

**Definition:** Flexible straps worn around the waist to hold up clothing or as a fashion accessory.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Dress/Casual:** leather belt, reversible belt, dress belt, woven belt
- **Western:** rodeo belt, western belt, tooled leather, concho belt, decorative buckle
- **Work/Utility:** work belt, canvas belt, rigger belt, tactical belt
- **Features:** genuine leather, embossed, studded, engraved, branded buckle

**Key Characteristics:** Waist accessories made of leather, canvas, or fabric; features functional or decorative buckles; worn as clothing accessories.

---

### Scarves & Wraps

**Definition:** Fabric accessories worn around the neck, shoulders, or head for warmth, fashion, or sun protection.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Neck Accessories:** scarf, infinity scarf, neck gaiter, bandana, wild rag
- **Shoulder Wraps:** shawl, wrap, poncho, ruana, pashmina
- **Materials:** silk, wool, cashmere, fleece, knit, woven
- **Features:** fringe, tassels, printed patterns, solid colors

**Key Characteristics:** Textile accessories for personal adornment or warmth worn on the upper body, neck, or head.

---

### Sunglasses & Eyewear

**Definition:** Protective eyewear for sun protection, fashion, or safety in work and sports environments.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Fashion:** aviator, wayfarer, cat-eye, oversized, mirrored
- **Sport:** wraparound, polarized, sport sunglasses, performance eyewear
- **Safety:** safety glasses, shooting glasses, protective eyewear
- **Features:** UV protection, polarized lenses, anti-scratch, lightweight

**Key Characteristics:** Eyewear worn on the face for eye protection from sun, glare, or workplace hazards; ranges from fashion sunglasses to certified safety glasses.

---

### Handkerchiefs

**Definition:** Small fabric squares carried for personal hygiene or as fashion accessories.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Types:** handkerchief, pocket handkerchief, hanky, bandana (when used as handkerchief)
- **Materials:** cotton, linen, silk
- **Features:** embroidered, printed, solid white, monogrammed

**Key Characteristics:** Small fabric squares carried in pockets or bags for personal use; often coordinated with formal menswear.

---

### Key Chains

**Definition:** Small accessories for organizing and carrying keys, often with decorative or functional elements.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Basic:** keychain, key ring, key fob, carabiner clip, lanyard
- **Functional:** mini flashlight, bottle opener, mini tool
- **Safety:** pepper spray keychain, personal alarm
- **Decorative:** charm keychain, leather keychain, branded keychain

**Key Characteristics:** Portable accessories for organizing keys; may include miniature tools, safety devices, or decorative elements.

---

### Gloves & Mittens

**Definition:** Hand coverings worn for warmth, protection, or work purposes.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Winter/Warmth:** winter gloves, fleece gloves, insulated gloves, mittens, hand warmers
- **Work/Utility:** work gloves, leather gloves, cowhide gloves, deerskin gloves, driver gloves
- **Sports:** batting gloves, football gloves, golf gloves, ski gloves, shooting gloves
- **Materials:** leather, fleece, knit, waterproof, insulated, Thinsulate

**Key Characteristics:** Hand protection for various purposes including cold weather, manual work, and sports activities; materials range from leather to synthetic performance fabrics.

---

### Suspenders

**Definition:** Straps worn over the shoulders to hold up trousers or pants.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Types:** suspenders, braces, galluses, elastic suspenders
- **Work:** utility suspenders, heavy-duty suspenders, work suspenders
- **Materials:** leather, elastic, canvas
- **Features:** adjustable, clip-on, button-on

**Key Characteristics:** Over-the-shoulder straps that attach to pants; available in utility/work styles and fashion styles.

---

## Bags

### Travel & Luggage

**Definition:** Bags and cases designed for transporting personal belongings during travel.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Luggage:** suitcase, carry-on, rolling luggage, hardside luggage, checked bag
- **Travel Bags:** duffel bag, weekender bag, garment bag, toiletry bag
- **Backpacks:** travel backpack, hiking pack, daypack
- **Features:** wheeled, TSA-approved, expandable, lightweight

**Key Characteristics:** Bags specifically designed for packing and transporting clothing and personal items during travel; emphasizes portability and organization.

---

### Handbags

**Definition:** Fashion and functional bags carried by hand or over the shoulder for daily personal items.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Styles:** tote, crossbody, satchel, clutch, hobo bag, shoulder bag
- **Features:** leather, suede, fringe, concho, studded, embossed
- **Specialty:** concealed carry, RFID protection
- **Brands:** Designer handbags, western brands

**Key Characteristics:** Fashion-forward bags for carrying daily essentials; emphasizes style, materials, and design details.

---

### Wallets & Money Clips

**Definition:** Small accessories for organizing cash, credit cards, and identification.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Types:** bifold wallet, trifold wallet, checkbook wallet, money clip, card holder
- **Features:** leather, RFID blocking, slim/minimalist, front pocket
- **Styles:** western wallet, rodeo wallet, tooled leather

**Key Characteristics:** Compact organizers for currency, cards, and identification; ranges from traditional leather wallets to modern minimalist designs.

---

### Other Bags

**Definition:** Specialized bags for specific activities like sports, fitness, or everyday use.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Sports/Gym:** gym bag, duffel bag, sports bag, gear bag
- **School:** backpack, book bag, student backpack
- **Specialty:** drawstring bag, cinch sack

**Key Characteristics:** Functional bags designed for specific activities beyond travel or fashion.

---

## Clothing

### Underwear & Socks

#### Socks

**Definition:** Foot coverings worn inside shoes for comfort, warmth, and moisture management.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Lengths:** crew socks, ankle socks, no-show socks, over-the-calf, knee-high
- **Types:** athletic socks, boot socks, dress socks, hiking socks, compression socks
- **Features:** cushioned, moisture-wicking, merino wool, cotton, reinforced heel/toe
- **Specialty:** diabetic socks, heated socks, thermal socks

**Key Characteristics:** Essential footwear accessories in various lengths and materials; categorized by activity (athletic, work, dress) and height.

---

#### Underwear

**Definition:** Undergarments worn next to the skin beneath outer clothing.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Men's:** boxer briefs, boxers, briefs, trunks, compression shorts
- **Women's:** bikini, hipster, briefs, thong, boy shorts
- **Thermal:** long johns, thermal underwear, base layer bottoms
- **Features:** cotton, moisture-wicking, tagless, comfort waistband

**Key Characteristics:** Foundation garments for personal comfort; includes everyday underwear and thermal base layers.

---

#### Other Underwear & Socks

**Definition:** Miscellaneous undergarments and hosiery not classified in specific categories.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Multi-packs:** value packs, variety packs
- **Specialty:** seamless, performance fabric, anti-odor

**Key Characteristics:** Additional undergarment items and accessories.

---

#### Slips & Shapewear

**Definition:** Compression and shaping undergarments designed to smooth body contours and provide support.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Products:** shapewear, body shaper, compression garment, slimming bodysuit, control shorts
- **Features:** tummy control, high waist, smoothing, compression, open bust
- **Brands:** Spanx, Maidenform

**Key Characteristics:** Supportive undergarments designed to shape and smooth the body's appearance; worn under fitted clothing.

---

#### Lingerie

**Definition:** Intimate apparel designed for aesthetics and comfort, including decorative undergarments and sleepwear.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Items:** babydoll, camisole, chemise, slip, teddy
- **Features:** lace, satin, mesh, embroidered, sheer
- **Materials:** silk, satin, lace, mesh

**Key Characteristics:** Decorative and feminine intimate apparel emphasizing aesthetics and delicate materials.

---

#### Hosiery & Tights

**Definition:** Leg coverings including sheer hosiery, opaque tights, and athletic compression legwear.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Fashion:** pantyhose, tights, stockings, control top, sheer, opaque
- **Athletic:** compression tights, workout tights, performance leggings
- **Equestrian:** riding tights, tek tights
- **Features:** reinforced toe, seamless, moisture-wicking

**Key Characteristics:** Form-fitting leg coverings ranging from delicate sheer hosiery to athletic compression wear.

---

#### Bras

**Definition:** Women's undergarments designed to support and shape the bust.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Types:** sports bra, t-shirt bra, push-up bra, wireless bra, strapless bra, bralette
- **Features:** padded, lined, seamless, convertible straps, racerback
- **Specialty:** nursing bra, maternity bra, adhesive bra

**Key Characteristics:** Supportive undergarments with varying levels of support, coverage, and styling for different activities.

---

#### Undershirts

**Definition:** Lightweight shirts worn under dress shirts or as base layers.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Types:** undershirt, tank undershirt, crew neck undershirt, V-neck undershirt
- **Features:** cotton, tagless, moisture-wicking, slim fit
- **Packs:** multi-pack, value pack

**Key Characteristics:** Basic shirts worn as a layer under other clothing for comfort and moisture absorption.

---

- **Patterns:** plaid, checkered, gingham, solid

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Styles:** polo shirt, golf polo, performance polo, pique polo
- **Features:** moisture-wicking, breathable, quick-dry, stretch
- **Purpose:** golf, casual wear, business casual

---

#### T-Shirts & Tank Tops

**Definition:** Casual, short-sleeved or sleeveless tops without collars or buttons.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Styles:** t-shirt, tee, crew neck, V-neck, tank top, pocket tee
- **Features:** cotton, moisture-wicking, heavyweight, solid colors

**Key Characteristics:** Most casual upper-body garment; basic solid colors and simple designs without prominent graphics.

---

#### Graphic Tees

**Definition:** T-shirts featuring printed graphics, logos, brand imagery, or artistic designs.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Content:** graphic t-shirt, logo tee, printed tee, branded tee

- **Features:** screen print, distressed print, vintage graphic
- **Brands:** Buck Wear, Grunt Style, Carhartt, Ariat, sports brands

**Key Characteristics:** Casual t-shirts distinguished by prominent graphics, logos, or printed designs; popular for expressing interests, brand affiliation, or personal style.

---

#### Blouses

**Definition:** Women's upper-body garments with feminine styling, often featuring decorative details.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Styles:** blouse, peasant blouse, button-down shirt, tunic, henley
- **Details:** ruffle, lace, embroidered, smocked, tie-front
- **Necklines:** V-neck, scoop neck, round neck, keyhole
- **Sleeves:** long sleeve, 3/4 sleeve, cap sleeve, flutter sleeve

**Key Characteristics:** Women's tops with refined or decorative styling; ranges from casual to dressy; distinguished from basic t-shirts by feminine details.

---

#### Bodysuits

**Definition:** One-piece garments that cover the torso and fasten at the crotch, worn as tops or as baby clothing.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Fashion:** bodysuit, tank bodysuit, sleeveless bodysuit, turtleneck bodysuit
- **Baby/Infant:** onesie, baby bodysuit, snap bodysuit, long-sleeve bodysuit
- **Features:** snap closure, stretchy, fitted, seamless

**Key Characteristics:** Form-fitting tops that snap at the crotch for a tucked-in look; also includes infant onesies and baby bodysuits.

---

#### Sweaters & Cardigans

**Definition:** Knitted upper-body garments for warmth and layering.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Styles:** pullover sweater, cardigan, quarter-zip, turtleneck, V-neck sweater
- **Knits:** cable knit, ribbed, chunky knit, fine knit
- **Materials:** wool, cotton, fleece, merino wool

**Key Characteristics:** Warm, knitted garments for layering; ranges from casual to semi-formal styles.

### Bottoms

#### Skirts

**Definition:** Lower-body garments that hang from the waist without dividing into separate leg coverings.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Lengths:** mini skirt, midi skirt, maxi skirt, knee-length
- **Styles:** A-line, pencil skirt, pleated, tiered, wrap skirt
- **Athletic:** tennis skort, golf skort (skirt with built-in shorts)

**Key Characteristics:** Feminine lower-body garment; includes dressy skirts, casual skirts, and athletic skorts.

---

#### Shorts

**Definition:** Lower-body garments that end above the knee for warm weather or athletic activities.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Styles:** cargo shorts, denim shorts, chino shorts, athletic shorts
- **Work:** work shorts, utility shorts, canvas shorts
- **Features:** pockets, stretch, elastic waist, belt loops

**Key Characteristics:** Above-knee length bottoms for casual wear, work, or sports.

---

#### Jeans

**Definition:** Denim pants in various styles and fits for casual and work wear.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Fits:** relaxed fit, bootcut, straight leg, slim fit, skinny, tapered, athletic fit
- **Styles:** cowboy cut, western jeans, carpenter jeans, work jeans, fashion jeans
- **Features:** stretch, flex, comfort waist, high-rise, mid-rise, low-rise
- **Brands:** Wrangler, Levi's, Cinch, Ariat, Silver Jeans

**Key Characteristics:** Classic denim pants; categorized by fit (relaxed, slim, bootcut) and purpose (fashion, work, western); a staple of casual American wardrobe.

---

#### Pants

**Definition:** Non-denim trousers for work, casual, dress, and outdoor activities.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Work:** work pants, cargo pants, carpenter pants, canvas pants, duck pants
- **Casual:** chinos, khakis, joggers, sweatpants
- **Dress:** dress pants, slacks, trousers, pleated pants
- **Outdoor:** hiking pants, softshell pants, snow pants, rain pants
- **Features:** stretch, relaxed fit, flat front, pleated, rugged flex

**Key Characteristics:** Full-length lower-body garments excluding denim jeans; ranges from formal dress pants to durable work pants and athletic joggers.

---

#### Other Bottoms

**Definition:** Additional lower-body garments not classified as jeans, pants, shorts, or skirts.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Specialty:** leggings, yoga pants, overalls, bib overalls, coveralls
- **Features:** stretch, elastic waist, full-length

**Key Characteristics:** Miscellaneous full-length bottoms including casual leggings and workwear overalls.

---

### Uniforms

**Definition:** Standardized clothing for work, medical, sports, or institutional purposes.

**Distinguishing Keywords:**
- **Medical:** scrubs, scrub top, scrub pants, medical uniform
- **Work:** coveralls, overalls, bib overalls, work pants
- **Features:** flame-resistant (FR), high-visibility, reinforced

**Key Characteristics:** Functional, standardized clothing for specific occupations; prioritizes durability and safety.

---

### Dresses, Jumpsuits, & Rompers

- **Styles:** sundress, shift dress, fit-and-flare, wrap dress, shirt dress
- **Features:** sleeveless, short sleeve, long sleeve, floral print, tiered

### Boots
